Welcome to Katane Blitz, a fast-paced bomb defusal project where your knowledge will be put to the test! No downloads required; just play!

Endless Chapter

The main experience of Katane Blitz. Randomly generated modules! Time extensions are granted with each module, but become less effective as modules are solved.
Play Endless Challenge!

Needy modules too stressful? Endless Stable has got your back... at the expense of starting a mission with 2 minutes less on the clock.
Play Endless Stable!

Endless Hardcore is for players/teams who think they have mastered the art of defusing. One misstep will end these games!
Play Endless Hardcore!!!

Short Chapter

This chapter allows playing missions of a relatively fixed length. Fair warning; the timer will still count down faster if you incur a strike.
minutes Play mission!

Practice Chapter

Practice 1 or more modules until the required disarms are scored, or a bomb explodes.
Big Button Complicated Wires Keypad Maze Memory Morse Code Password Simon Says Who's on First Wires Wire Sequence Mixed Practice

Needy Chapter

Needy modules pose a constant threat, but will auto-disarm once all other modules are solved. These games have goals of 15 solvable + 6 needy modules.
Venting Gas Threatening Capacitor Knobbing Away Mixed Needies

Large Chapter

These single bombs are huge. In return, solved modules are also hidden.

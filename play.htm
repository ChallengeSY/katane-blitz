Instructions

The rules of Katane Blitz are somewhat simple. The player is dealt a series of randomly generated modules, and the objective is to solve each one, without incurring strikes (causing a life reduction). A game ends when the life or timer pools empty out. A game is won if the required score goal (if specified) can be reached.

Arcade games will generate an unlimited number of modules. Endless games grant time extensions with each module solved, but the amount of time granted with each solve decreases as more modules are scored. Short games have a fixed initial length.
